The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation
Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.
Green Energy
Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.
Modern Infrastructure
Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global
competitiveness.

"Dr. Muhammad Yunus envisions a future for Bangladesh driven by innovation and social entrepreneurship. He believes the country can become a leader in tackling global challenges through sustainable solutions, technology, and education. By focusing on financial inclusion, empowering women, and fostering entrepreneurship, Yunus sees Bangladesh emerging as a hub for impactful solutions that create jobs, improve lives, and contribute to global progress, making a significant mark on both the economy and society."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
